About the retreat

4 days, 3 nights yoga and meditation retreat in a beautiful luxurious villa surrounded by nature in Costa Brava. You will enjoy nature while practicing outdoor ashtanga, vinyasa yoga, yogilates and meditation. Between yoga classes you can spend time discovering the nice beaches of Costa Brava, visiting medival towns and fine wineries of Empordà or enjoying art at Dalí Museum in Figueres.

Includes pick up and drop off at the train station in Figueres (AVE train takes less than an hour from Barcelona) , accommodation in beautifully decorated rooms with private bathroom, all meals and snacks.
